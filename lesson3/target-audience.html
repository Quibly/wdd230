Target Audience

The target audiences for the site are businesses, community members, and tourists. Providing opportunties for businesses to interact and help one another, it is hoped that commerce will be improved. Providing additional information about community events and recreational opportunities will help community members find more enjoyment while encouraging additional commerce for tourists visiting the area for the community events.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: What kind of businesses are affiliated with the Vernal Chamber?

  • Newest business associations include Vernal Hay Company, Adventure Tours Vernal, Main Street Tax & Accounting.

Scenario 2: How can the Vernal Chamber help me and my business?

  • Chamber membership includes online advertising, access to the chamber network of businesses, and involvement in local community events.

Scenario 3: What kind of recreational activities are available in the Vernal area?

  • Whether you are looking for an outdoor adventure in a forest, riverside location, lakeside environment, or desert scenary, Vernal has you covered in any direction.

Scenario 4: When are community activities happening?

  • You can check out or community event calendar. It will stear you towards ongoing events for the whole family.

Scenario 5: I need to know more about the community in Vernal

  • Check out our Come to Vernal page. There are many references to the varying aspects of our community.
