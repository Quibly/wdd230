Site Purpose

There is a rich history and heritage involved with Vernal, Utah. The purpose of this site is to explore the opportunities available for businesses, citizens, tourists, and the area. By promoting Vernal's history, existing infistructure, and possibilities, it is hoped that many will be inspired to promote growth in the region.
