Weddings and Temple Missionaries

Wedding Reception Services

There can be a lot of choices to make when you're preparing a wedding reception. Our packages are designed to make it easier so you can enjoy your special day. Tell us your wedding colors and we have 3 options designed to accomodate various budgets and group arrangements:

  • Table Services

    If you are looking for all the convenience you can find for your guests, then this would be the right option. We'll prepare the reception hall to match your color preferences and provide a menu table service for all of your guests to sit and relax.

  • Family Services

    The Family Services are a hybrid between full table services and the buffet service option. With this option your guests will be served family sized portions of menu items for all to share at the table.

  • Buffet Services

    With Buffet Services, your guests can eat and grab food at their convenience. This is a favorite option for those looking to save some money on waitstaff, while still providing flavorful options for their guests.
