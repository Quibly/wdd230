Vernal Demographics

Vernal is a city located in Uintah County Utah. It is also the county seat of Uintah County. With a 2020 population of 10,657, it is the 62nd largest city in Utah and the 2978th largest city in the United States .

Vernal is currently growing at a rate of 0.69% annually and its population has increased by 17.25% since the most recent census, which recorded a population of 9,089 in 2010.

Spanning over 5 miles, Vernal has a population density of 2,306 people per square mile.

The average household income in Vernal is $63,338 with a poverty rate of 20.34%. The median rental costs in recent years comes to $732 per month, and the median house value is $166,400. The median age in Vernal is 30.8 years, 30 years for males, and 32.4 years for females.
